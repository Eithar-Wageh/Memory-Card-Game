Magic Cards

Chosen in settings (timer: 10/15/20s). Appear one by one every timer interval. Max: 2 (4x4), 4 (6x6), 6 (8x8). Match in 7s for 20 points, reveal all (2s), or freeze time (5s). Match shakes after 2s if unflipped.

Spam Cards

Bombs explode in 3s, causing: shuffle (cards swap) or flip (matched cards hide).

Score

10 for normal match, 15 for consecutive, 20 for magic. Doubled if fast.

Settings

Choose theme, sound, special cards (classic/magic/spam), timer, and mode (classic/timed/moves).
